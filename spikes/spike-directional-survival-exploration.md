# Derived: Directional Survival Exploration (LMI Constraint)

The heuristic unified objective ( #disc-ciy-unified-objective) posits a scalar trade-off $\lambda \cdot \text{CIY}$ to balance pragmatic value against exploration. This segment derives the exact, multi-dimensional form of this objective. By formulating survival as a Linear Matrix Inequality (LMI) on the agent's epistemic precision, we prove that exploration is the exact KKT shadow price of the Lyapunov persistence condition. Furthermore, it proves that exploration must be *directional*: the agent must gather information specifically aligned with the eigenvectors of the environmental drift.

## Formal Expression

### The Precision LMI

Let the agent's epistemic state be a Gaussian belief with precision matrix $P_t = \Sigma_t^{-1}$. The environment undergoes an additive random walk disturbance with covariance $Q_\rho$.

In the information-filter formulation of the optimal Bayesian update, the precision after an environmental drift and a subsequent observation is:
$$ P_{t+1} = (P_t^{-1} + Q_\rho)^{-1} + \mathcal{I}_o(a_t) $$
where $\mathcal{I}_o(a_t)$ is the Fisher Information Matrix generated by taking action $a_t$. As established in #def-causal-information-yield, $\mathcal{I}_o(a_t)$ is the formal multi-dimensional generalization of Causal Information Yield (CIY).

To satisfy the structural persistence condition ( #result-persistence-condition), the agent's uncertainty must remain bounded within its capacity $R$. Specifically, its precision must stay above a critical floor: $P_{t+1} \succeq P_{\text{crit}} = R^{-2} I$.

Assume the agent is currently at the boundary of survival ($P_t = P_{\text{crit}}$). To prevent fatal destabilization in the next step, the action $a_t$ must generate enough Fisher information to compensate for the drift $Q_\rho$:
$$ (P_{\text{crit}}^{-1} + Q_\rho)^{-1} + \mathcal{I}_o(a_t) \succeq P_{\text{crit}} $$

Rearranging isolates the minimum required information matrix:
$$ \mathcal{I}_o(a_t) \succeq P_{\text{crit}} - (P_{\text{crit}}^{-1} + Q_\rho)^{-1} := \mathcal{I}_{\text{req}}(Q_\rho, R) $$

Because $(P_{\text{crit}}^{-1} + Q_\rho) \succ P_{\text{crit}}^{-1}$, its inverse is strictly less than $P_{\text{crit}}$, ensuring $\mathcal{I}_{\text{req}}$ is a symmetric positive-definite matrix. This is the **Survival LMI**: the agent's chosen action *must* yield a Fisher Information Matrix that dominates $\mathcal{I}_{\text{req}}$ in the positive-semidefinite cone.

### The Trace-Lagrangian Objective

The agent seeks to maximize its pragmatic objective $Q_O(a)$ subject to this survival constraint. This forms a semidefinite program:
$$ \max_{a} Q_O(a) $$
$$ \text{s.t. } \mathcal{I}_o(a) - \mathcal{I}_{\text{req}} \succeq 0 $$

By the generalized Karush-Kuhn-Tucker (KKT) conditions, the Lagrangian is:
$$ \mathcal{L}(a, \Lambda) = Q_O(a) + \text{Tr}\left( \Lambda \left[ \mathcal{I}_o(a) - \mathcal{I}_{\text{req}} \right] \right) $$
where $\Lambda \succeq 0$ is the dual variable matrix (the shadow price of survival).

Dropping the constants with respect to $a$, the agent's exact optimal action-selection rule is:
$$ a_t^\ast = \arg\max_a \left[ Q_O(a) + \text{Tr}\left( \Lambda \cdot \mathcal{I}_o(a) \right) \right] $$

## Discussion

**Resolution of the "Blank Wall" Paradox.** If the objective were a simple scalar trace constraint ($\text{Tr}(\mathcal{I}_o) \ge c$), an agent could "survive" on paper by taking trivial actions that yield massive amounts of useless information about static features of the environment (e.g., staring at a blank wall), while remaining completely blind to the dimensions where the environment is actively drifting ($Q_\rho$). 

The matrix formulation prohibits this. The shadow price matrix $\Lambda$ has its largest eigenvalues aligned with the eigenvectors of $\mathcal{I}_{\text{req}}$, which in turn align with the principal components of the environmental drift $Q_\rho$. If an action yields information orthogonal to the drift, $\text{Tr}(\Lambda \cdot \mathcal{I}_o(a)) \approx 0$. The agent receives no exploration bonus. The math *forces* the agent to ask the specific causal questions that neutralize the specific threats to its survival.

**Replacement of the Heuristic.** This derivation replaces the scalar heuristic $Q_O + \lambda \cdot \text{CIY}$ with an exact, tensor-valued physical law. The exploration weight $\Lambda$ is not an arbitrary hyperparameter tuned by the designer; it is the exact, dynamically computed shadow price of the boundary separating persistence from death.
